About Event

Squid Saga: A Coding Fiesta is a national level coding competition for students held under Pioneer Event of KIT's College of Engineering, Kolhapur.

ACSES began with a mindset of encouraging beginner coders to experience competitive programming & focused to improve problem solving skills.

Where

KIT's College of Engineering, Kolhapur

Event Schedule

Full Vaccination is mandatory as competition will be held in Offline Mode in KIT's College of Engineering, Kolhapur.

9:30 AM - 10:15 AM

Inauguration

Inauguration will be held at the venue of KIT's College of Engineering, Kolhapur. Please carry your college ID card (mandatory).

10:30 AM - 11:30 AM

Round 1: Age of Aptitude (Aptitude Test)

The participants will be given a set of MCQ questions and they have to answer them in the given time limit of 60 minutes. The questions will be of various types(technical, quantitative).

12:30 PM - 1:15 PM

Round 2: Pseudo War

This round contains various types of questions like Bug Hunting, Error Detecting, Pseudo Code, etc.

2:15 PM - 3:15 PM

Round 3: Endgame (Coding)

This round contains multiple coding questions based on difficulty levels. The participant will have to write code in online IDE & run to get desired output.

4:00 PM - 4:45 PM

Closing Ceremony

Prize distribution will be done in closing ceremony.
