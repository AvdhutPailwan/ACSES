About Us

Our Vision

ACSES began with a mindset of encouraging beginner coders to experience competitive programming & focused to improve problem solving skills. Our vision is to help students nurture and grow their interpersonal and technical skills. We provide platform for students to show their coding skills by organizing various coding competitions every year. We also organize technical workshops based on various programming languages.
